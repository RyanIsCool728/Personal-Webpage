Among Us Info!

Among Us is a fun murder mystery game where you try to find the inposter with your party which varies from 3-10.

Among us is a game of teamwork and sometimes even betrayal. Crewmates complete tasks and try not to get killed by the imposter.

In my opinion, Among Us is very fun because you get to spend time with friends and family and that's what it is all about.
