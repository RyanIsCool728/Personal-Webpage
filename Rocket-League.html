Rocket League Info!

Rocket League is a free-to-play fun game where you can play with friends, scoring goals on each other in multiple different modes.

The modes in Rocket League are Soccar, Snow Day, Hoops, Rumble, Dropshot, Casual, and Competitive

In Rocket League there is what is called the "Rocket Pass" and it is just like the battle pass in fortnite just with styles for your car in game.

There is a limited-time collab with the NFL in Rocket League and there is a new mode and limited skins for your car. The mode is just like
a normal match, but now there are Footballs. Also when you score you get 7 points now!

Rocket League is always getting updated, with new ways to make the game fun, so you should try it if you haven't already!
