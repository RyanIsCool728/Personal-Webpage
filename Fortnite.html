Fortnite Info!

Here you will find everything up to date about Fortnite!

Fortnite is currently in Version 1.30. Dinosaurs and tornados are still in the game,
but Epic games added The Foundation along with his unmasked emote, and other
cosmetics of his. They have also added Haven's Masks. If you collect enough feathers,
you can purchase different masks for Haven.

Fortnite's current battle pass includes Ronin, Haven, Gumbo, Harlowe, Lt. John Llama,
Shanta, Spider Man, and The Foundation.

to get you leveled up and get the new battle pass skins quicker!
