Top 5 Xbox Games!

Top 5 Games for cool people!

I will be creating an unordered list for the description and I will be making an
ordered list for the games in order from one to 5

  • Fortnite: Fortnite is a third person shooter game, where you can play battle royale where the objective
    is to be the last to survive to earn a victory royale. Other modes are creative and floor is lava.
  • Fifa 22: Fifa 22 is a realistic soccer game where there are several modes such as kickoff where you
    play the game with regular rules or you can play no rules where you can slidetackle all you want
    to get the ball, without getting called.
  • Halo Infinite: Halo Infinite is a third person shooter(Just like Fortnite) and there are 2 modes,
    either the free-to-play multiplayer, or the campain. (Which you have to pay money for)
  • Rocket League: Rocket League is a third person game, where you are a car and the objective is
    to use your car to hit the gigantic ball into the goal.(to score points)
  • Among Us: Among Us Is a third-person game where you try to find the imposter with you and
    the people in your server. It is very difficult to be the imposter because you need to make sure
    that you eliminate people in private and that you keep a low profile.
  1. Fortnite
  2. Halo Infinite
  3. Rocket League
  4. Among Us
  5. Fifa 22
